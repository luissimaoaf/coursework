american

This module showcases the pricing methods for American-style calls and puts.

Files:
- bsm_formula.py
    Implements the BSM formula for use in the other scripts.

- binomial.py
    Implements the binomial, binomial BS and the BBSR methods.

- approx.py
    Implements the analytical approximation of Barone-Adesi and Whaley.

- integral.py
    Implements the integral representation approach of Kim.
    Allows for the computation of the early exercise boundary as well.

- script.py
    Performs the computations for the comparison of the methods as requested in
    exercise 6(d), using the previously mentioned scripts.
